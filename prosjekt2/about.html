Welcome to the official website of Re-Cycle Tours (City, Cultural and Ecological Tours, known locally as Re-Cycle). Famed for the original eco/educational cycling tour since 1999, we proudly ensure visitors see the 'Real city' with our range of Cycling Tours

Cycling

Bicycle touring means self-contained cycling trips for pleasure, adventure, and autonomy rather than sport, commuting, or exercise. Touring can range from single-to multi-day trips, even years. Tours may be planned by the participant or organised by a holiday business, a club, or a charity as a fund-raising venture.

Community

A social group of any size whose members reside in a specific locality. Cycling, also called bicycling or biking, is the use of bicycles for transport, recreation, exercise or sport.
